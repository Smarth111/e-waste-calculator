Retired EV Batteries Solutions

Second-life Applications

  • Energy Storage Systems (ESS)

    Repurpose old EV batteries for solar/wind energy storage, providing sustainable power solutions.

  • Backup Power for Buildings

    Use them in UPS systems or grid backup solutions to provide emergency power.

  • Low-power Mobility

    Give batteries new life in electric scooters, forklifts, or golf carts.

Battery Refurbishment & Recycling

  • Battery Refurbishment

    Extract healthy cells and rebuild into smaller batteries for various applications.

  • Recycling & Material Recovery

    Use advanced recycling techniques to recover valuable materials like lithium, nickel, cobalt, and copper.

  • Manufacturer Take-Back Programs

    Mandate or incentivize manufacturers to collect and recycle old batteries.
